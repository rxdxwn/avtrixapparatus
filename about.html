Who We Are

Avtrix Apparatus Trading FZE is an emerging, specialized trading startup established in 2026 in the United Arab Emirates under the leadership of owner Jaydip Ratibhai Lakhani.

We focus squarely on the B2B supply of specialized industrial machinery, precise spare parts, workshop equipment, and specialized engines. Our primary vertical is dedicated to the rapidly evolving Lab-Grown Diamond (LGD) manufacturing industry.

While our enterprise launched in 2026, our foundation is built upon years of intensive, on-the-ground industry experience and deep ties within the UAE's prominent diamond free zones.
